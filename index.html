FAQ.
How do I place an order?

Reach out via Instagram or Telegram with your size and preferred look. We'll confirm availability and take it from there.

How is payment handled?

50% advance at booking, remaining balance on delivery. UPI, bank transfer, and cash all accepted.

Can pieces be custom tailored to my size?

Yes — all garments are made-to-measure. Share your measurements and we'll craft the perfect fit for you.

What is the delivery time?

Standard orders take 10–15 working days. Express options may be available for an additional charge.

Do you offer styling guidance?

Absolutely. After booking we'll share styling tips, accessory suggestions, and outfit pairings tailored to the occasion.
